About Me

Hi, I'm Umberto!

I am a Computer Science graduate and Game Programming student, interested in Gameplay and AI development. I mainly work on Unreal Engine 5, building prototypes through game jams and personal projects, always trying to challenge myself with new ideas and new technical problems. I'm currently strengthening my skills in C++, to expand my knowledge to lower-level game programming and to gain a clearer understanding of what runs beneath a game engine.

Playing memorable games has given me some of my best experiences and inspired my career goal: to help create games that move and engage players the way those titles moved me. This motivates me to learn continuously, collaborate across disciplines, and embrace the challenges of international work.

Feel free to contact me for project collaborations or playtest exchanges.
